STEP 1: Understanding the Big Question

Central Historical Question: How did Americans during the Revolutionary era understand and practice a key democratic norm, and what do its successes or failures reveal about the consequences of the American Revolution?
Think about what it means to study a democratic norm through the lens of history.

STEP 2: Establishing Historical Context

Be specific! Example: "The debate over who should have the right to vote in the new state constitutions"
