History Lab Research Project

Central Question: How did this raw material become a global commodity, and what does that process reveal about capitalism, empire, and globalization?

Components:

  • Curated Mini-Archive (8-12 sources)
  • Archive Rationale (750-1000 words)
  • Concept Map or Global Flow Diagram
  • Visual Research Poster

Citation Style: Turabian

Due: Week 15 | Value: 20% of Final Grade
