Step 3 of 4 — Paper Outline

📋 What to do for Check-In #3

Build a detailed outline for each section of your paper. For every major section, you need:

  • A topic sentence stating the main argument of that section
  • Key evidence — specific sources, documents, or Pelz chapters you'll cite
  • At least 1 specific primary source identified for each main body section
  • Brief notes on the analytical points you'll develop

Goal: By the end of this step, you should be able to see where your argument is strong and where you need more research.
