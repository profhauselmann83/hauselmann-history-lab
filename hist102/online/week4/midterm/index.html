Document 1 of 3

Source A

"We meet in the midst of a nation brought to the verge of moral, political, and material ruin. Corruption dominates the ballot-box, the Legislatures, the Congress, and touches even the ermine of the bench. The newspapers are largely subsidized or muzzled, public opinion silenced, business prostrated, homes covered with mortgages, labor impoverished, and the land concentrating in the hands of capitalists."

Source: Political party platform statement, 1892. American political document.

Directions
Examine the photograph below and answer the questions that follow.
"Migrant Mother"  ·  Dorothea Lange  ·  February 1936  ·  Library of Congress

The Resettlement Administration was one of President Franklin Roosevelt's New Deal agencies that helped people displaced by the Dust Bowl. The agency hired photographer Dorothea Lange to document conditions in migrant labor camps and build public support for its programs. This photograph was one of several Lange took of migrant farm worker Florence Thompson, age 32, and two of her seven children at a pea-pickers camp in Nipomo, California.

Source: Dorothea Lange, "Migrant Mother," February 1936. Farm Security Administration / Office of War Information Photograph Collection, Library of Congress. Public domain.
