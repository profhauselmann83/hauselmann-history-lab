What this lecture covers This week's lecture is organized around three questions:
  1. Why did the United States turn outward? — the logic of imperialism, the Spanish-American War, and the debate over empire
  2. Who were the Progressives, and what did they want? — muckrakers, settlement houses, TR's Square Deal, Wilson's New Freedom, the four reform amendments
  3. What did "progress" mean for Black Americans? — Jim Crow, the Great Migration, Washington vs. Du Bois, and the founding of the NAACP
